Internet of Things (IOT)

The Internet of Things(IOT), is where different devices or objects connect to the internet or private networks. This can be done via WiFi, Bluetooth etc.

This gives you the ability to control and monitor these devices or objects from a single point, from anywhere in the world.

The application of this is endless and can be used from the normal household, up to the largest business or industries.
